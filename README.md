# U7-IW cover

![Image of cover on U7-IW on a table top. The ports are exposed under the cover.](/assets/DSC06848.jpg)

This is a 3D printable cover for the Ubiquiti U7 In-Wall. It accommodates access to the 2.5 GbE ports but not to the LED or reset button.

The cover snaps on by angled hooks around the perimeter. It has a tight but accurate fit - gently pull on the sides when installing until they slip onto the AP, then press completely in. It will be pulled flush with the wall.

For this reason the cover must have some flex to bend during install. Consider this when choosing print material or scale up slightly.

![Image of underside of the cover. Its hooks are shown holding it securely to the U7-IW with wall plate installed.](/assets/DSC06850.jpg)

The walls of the cover are 1.8mm thick. This and other adjustments can be made in the included .blend file.
